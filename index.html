About

TAMS Computer Science Organization is a club dedicated to improving ability in computer science. We focus on everything related to computer science, including algorithms, web design, game programming, and more.

GAs

Our club meets bi-weekly on Mondays and Thursdays at 8:00 PM in the computer lab. Mondays are reserved for algorithms, and Thursdays are for the GA and beginner lessons. NOTE: These dates are subject to change
